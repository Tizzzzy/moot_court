🎯 WHAT WAS ACCOMPLISHED

Three major enhancements were implemented to transform the court simulator
into an intelligent learning platform:

1️⃣  EVIDENCE MEMORY FIX
    Problem: Judge forgot evidence after a few turns
    Solution: Retrieve all evidence from persistent storage on every turn
    Result: Judge now has full evidence context in verdicts

2️⃣  PLAINTIFF COACHING FEEDBACK
    Problem: No way for plaintiffs to learn courtroom performance
    Solution: Generate constructive feedback after every statement
    Result: Users receive specific coaching tied to their statements

3️⃣  EDUCATIONAL OBJECTION MONITORING
    Problem: Users only learn about objections when they fail
    Solution: Alert when plaintiff could object to defendant's statements
    Result: Users learn offensive strategy through opponent's mistakes

💡 KEY INSIGHTS

Evidence Memory:
  Insight: Evidence was already persistent; just needed consistent retrieval.
  Solution: Always read from persistent store, not temporary buffer.

Plaintiff Feedback:
  Insight: Learning is most effective when immediate and specific.
  Solution: Generate tailored feedback after every statement.

Educational Objections:
  Insight: Users learn best from opponent mistakes.
  Solution: Highlight objection opportunities without disrupting trial.
